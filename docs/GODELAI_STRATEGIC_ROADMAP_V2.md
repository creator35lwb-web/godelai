## Proposed Strategic Roadmap v2.0

### Phase 1: The C-S-P Diagnostic Toolkit (Q1 2026)

**Goal**: Transform the C-S-P framework from a theoretical model into a practical, revenue-generating diagnostic tool.

**Why**: The conversation ended with a challenge: apply C-S-P to a real industry or create a product/consulting framework. This is the fastest path to demonstrating real-world value and generating the resources needed for long-term model development.

**Key Deliverables**:

1.  **C-S-P Audit Framework**: A structured methodology to diagnose any organization's "Propagation Capability."
    -   **Compression Audit**: Is knowledge explicit and structured, or trapped in human minds?
    -   **State Audit**: Are processes dependent on individuals, or are they institutionalized?
    -   **Propagation Audit**: Can core competencies be high-fidelity replicated to the next generation of employees/systems?

2.  **GodelAI-Internal**: A specialized version of our SLM, fine-tuned on business, management, and systems-thinking texts. Its purpose is to assist in the C-S-P audit process, analyzing internal company documents (reports, emails, process docs) to identify C-S-P weaknesses.

3.  **Pilot Program**: Engage with 3-5 friendly organizations (startups, non-profits, or even departments within larger companies) to conduct the first C-S-P audits. This will provide case studies and testimonials.

**Success Metric**: Successfully generate the first dollar of revenue from a C-S-P audit. This proves the model has real-world economic value.

### Phase 2: The Propagation Engine (Q2-Q3 2026)

**Goal**: Build the core GodelAI model and the YSenseAI data pipeline, using the revenue and insights from Phase 1.

**Key Deliverables**:

1.  **YSenseAI v1.0**: A functional data pipeline for sourcing, cleaning, and attributing "wisdom data." Initially, this can be from public domain sources (classic literature, philosophical texts, scientific papers) but architected for future user-generated content.

2.  **GodelAI v0.1 (Codename: "Zarathustra")**: The first iteration of our SLM trained on YSenseAI data. The architecture will be optimized for C-S-P metrics:
    -   **Loss Function**: Includes `L_propagation` as a core component.
    -   **Metrics**: Explicitly tracks Compression Quality, State Health, and Propagation Bandwidth during training.

3.  **VerifiMind-PEAS v2.0**: An updated version of the PEAS framework that uses the C-S-P metrics from our GodelAI integration as a core part of its validation suite.

**Success Metric**: Demonstrate that GodelAI v0.1, when audited by the C-S-P Diagnostic Toolkit, scores significantly higher on Propagation Capability than any off-the-shelf open-source SLM.

### Phase 3: The Open Source Ecosystem (Q4 2026)

**Goal**: Open source GodelAI and the C-S-P framework to the world, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem.

**Key Deliverables**:

1.  **Public Release of GodelAI**: Publish the model weights, training code, and all associated research on Hugging Face and GitHub.

2.  **C-S-P Foundation**: Launch a non-profit foundation or open-source collective dedicated to the C-S-P framework. This will house the diagnostic toolkit, research papers, and community resources.

3.  **Academic Partnerships**: Formalize partnerships with institutions like MIT Sloan and the Santa Fe Institute, who are already exploring these concepts.

**Success Metric**: The first external, independent research paper is published that uses or references the C-S-P framework or GodelAI.
